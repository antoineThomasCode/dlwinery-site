## Three Wine Tasting Experiences to Make Mom's Day

We offer three distinct **wine tasting for mom** experiences, each one a different way to say "thank you." Whether she is a seasoned wine lover or simply curious, there is an experience that fits her perfectly.

### Educational Wine Tasting -- $15 per person

Our guided tasting of five wines is the ideal introduction. Over about 40 minutes, one of our knowledgeable team members walks you through five carefully selected wines from our collection of 30+ artisanal bottles. She will learn how to identify aromas, understand what makes Finger Lakes Riesling so distinctive, and discover why our Chardonnays keep earning praise from visitors who know their wine.

This is wine education the way it should be: warm, conversational, and free of pretension. Questions are encouraged. Second favorites are absolutely allowed.

*Wine Club members taste free, plus up to three guests.*

### A Taste of France: Wine and Macaron Pairing -- $28 per person

If your mother loves the finer things but has a weakness for something sweet, this is her experience. Five wines paired with five French macarons, each combination chosen to play off complementary and opposing flavors in ways that genuinely surprise.

Picture the moment: a crisp Riesling meets a delicate passion fruit macaron, and the pairing unlocks something neither could achieve alone. That is the magic of French food and wine pairing -- and it is the kind of detail that makes a **mothers day winery experience** feel truly curated.

This seated experience is our most popular for Mother's Day. It is elegant without being stuffy, indulgent without being heavy, and perfectly suited for a group of two or a table of four.

![Wine and macaron pairing experience perfect for a Mother's Day wine gift](winery-wine-macaron.webp)

### Signature Food and Wine Pairing -- $35 per person

For the mother who appreciates a full sensory journey, our premium seated experience pairs five wines with five artisanal bites inspired by classic French flavors. Local cheeses, European charcuterie, and seasonal preparations that our team selects to showcase the unique relationship between food, wine, and terroir.

This is our most immersive tasting -- the one where conversations deepen, new favorites are discovered, and the afternoon stretches in the best possible way. If you are planning a **mother's day wine tasting** that she will describe as unforgettable, this is the one.
